Caring for Children with Special Health Care Needs (CHSCN) in Early Care and Education is a collection of 146 nationally recognized health and safety standards that have an impact on infants, toddlers, and preschoolers with special health care needs in early care and education settings. These materials and the associated six Appendices are a subset of materials available in Caring for Our Children: National Health and Safety Performance Standards; Guidelines for Early Care and Education Programs (CFOC). CFOC is a collection of nationally recognized best practice health and safety standards for the early care and education environment. The CSHCN collection is a compilation of best practices for children with special health care needs in early care and education programs. All CFOC standards are applicable to children with special healthcare care needs. However, this collection of standards, when put into practice, is best able to meet the specific needs of this vulnerable population.
